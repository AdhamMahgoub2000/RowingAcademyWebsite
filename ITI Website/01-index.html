The Information Technology Institute (ITI) is Egypt’s leading technology training center, established in 1993. It operates under the Ministry of Communications and Information Technology (MCIT) and focuses on preparing highly skilled ICT professionals to meet the country’s digital and technological needs. Over the past 30+ years, ITI has continuously updated its programs to align with global tech trends and national mega projects. It offers practical training, mentorship, and partnerships with both industry and academia, helping graduates achieve a high employment rate. ITI has trained thousands of Egyptian youth across software development, data science, cybersecurity, AI, cloud computing, and design fields.
