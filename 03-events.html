Our Events & Adventures

Beyond daily training, we organize spectacular team building activities, host premier competitions, and travel for unforgettable rowing trips across Egypt.

Team Building Events

Corporate Rowing Days

Rowing is the ultimate team sport. Synchronization, communication, and absolute trust are vital on the water. We bring these core elements to corporate teams seeking powerful bonding experiences.

  • No experience needed: Our expert coaches handle absolute beginners safely.
  • Customized programs: Half-day or full-day events tailored to your company's goals.
  • Mini-regattas: End the day with a friendly, high-energy race between departments.

Competitions Organizing

Partnering with Egypt's premier fitness festivals

Rowathon at Elfit Egypt

The Ultimate Indoor Rowing Challenge

As the official rowing partner for Elfit Egypt, the region's largest fitness competition, we organize and manage the intense "Rowathon" segment. Athletes from across the Middle East push their limits on the concept2 ergs in this grueling test of endurance, power, and mental toughness. We provide the equipment, the standardized judging, and the electric atmosphere.

Kayak Competition at Olympix

City Edge Development Partnership

Expanding beyond traditional rowing, we proudly organized the flagship Kayak Competition at Olympix, hosted by City Edge Development. This event brought together water sports enthusiasts for thrilling sprint and endurance races. Our team managed course layout, safety protocols, and race officiating.

2 Days Trip

Ismailia Canal Expedition

Ismailia, Egypt

A favorite among our masters and recreational rowers. Experience the calm, wide waters of the Ismailia canal. This weekend trip features long steady-state rows, technical focus sessions without the wake of the busy Cairo Nile, and wonderful seafood dinners by the water.
