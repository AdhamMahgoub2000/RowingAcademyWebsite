About Us

About US

Since 2017, we've been Cairo's premier rowing academy, dedicated to developing athletes and fostering a lifelong love of the sport.

Our Story

Rowin Academy was founded in 2017 by a group of passionate rowers who saw the need for a comprehensive rowing program on the Nile River. What started as a small club with just 15 members has grown into one of the most respected rowing academies on the Nile.

Over the past 9 years, we've trained over 2,000 athletes, produced numerous national champions, and sent several rowers to compete at the collegiate and Olympic levels. But our greatest achievement is building a community where rowers of all ages and abilities can pursue their passion for the sport.
